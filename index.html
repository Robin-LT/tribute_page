Baron Franz Nopcsa von Felső-Szilvás

The Father of Paleobiology

Notable Life Events:

  • Born on May 3rd 1877
  • Gave his first academic lecture at the age of 22
  • Gained a PhD in geology when aged 26
  • Worked as a weapons smuggler for the Albanian Nationalist Resistance who were fighting the Turks
  • Worked as a spy for Austria-Hungry during the Balkan Wars
  • Made a bid to become King of Albania once they gained their independance
  • Worked as a spy again for Austria-Hungary during the First World War
  • First person to hijack an aircraft
  • Commit murder-suicide on April 25th, 1933
